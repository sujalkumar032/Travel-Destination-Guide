Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1
Goa: Arrival and leisure day

As soon as you reach Goa, an agent's representative will greet and escort you to the hotel. Check-in to the hotel and relax. Remainder of the day is at leisure. Experience a laid back tropical vibe on this day. Go shopping in the local markets and savor authentic delights. Towards the end of the day, come back to the hotel for a restful sleep.

Day 2
Goa: Visit the lovely North Goa

A delectable breakfast begins your day, followed by a day trip to North Goa to visit its historical treasures and have a gala time at its beaches. Visit Aguada Fort and Calangute Annexe. Proceed to Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, and Anjuna Beach. After enjoying a great day as per your North Goa sightseeing package, come back to the hotel and slip into your bed for a soothing sleep.

Day 3
Goa: Have a great time in South Goa

'Break' your night long 'fast' with a sumptuous meal and set off to delight in the charm of South Goa. Visit Shri Shantadurga Temple, Shri Manguesh Temple, Basilica of Bom Jesus, and Se Cathedral. Drink in the beauty around of Mormugao Harbour from the Dona Paula Bay. Enjoy some time at Miramar Beach. When the sun sets, delight in a cruise on Mandovi River. After a happening day, come back to the hotel for a relaxing sleep.

Day 4
Goa: Go for an island tour

Arise to a nutritious breakfast and get ready to head to the Grand Island for a memorable experience. Reach Coco Beach jetty and reach Grand Island via boat ride. As you sail through the waters, spot dolphins and other marine life. Watch Millionaire Bungalow and Fort Aguada, and refuel yourself with a lip-smacking barbecue lunch before driving back to the jetty. Once the day has treated you well, return to the hotel and enjoy leisure time. Later, sleep peacefully at the hotel.

Day 5
Goa: Enjoy Dudhsagar waterfalls and spice plantations

Wake up to a toothsome morning meal and get ready to visit the glittering Dudhsagar Waterfalls and aromatic spice plantations. After delighting in the gushing cascade and aromatic plantations, return to the hotel for a restful sleep.

Day 6
Goa: Departure back home

Relish one more breakfast and checkout from the hotel. You will now be transferred to airport/railway station to board your flight/train back home.
